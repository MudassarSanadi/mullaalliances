Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, clear answers to common queries about our services, processes, and fees.

HomeFAQ
Income Tax
What documents are needed for ITR filing?
You will need Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), capital gains statements if applicable, Aadhaar, PAN, and last year's ITR acknowledgement. We provide a detailed checklist after your initial consultation.
Can you handle income tax notices on my behalf?
Yes, we handle all income tax notices including scrutiny assessments (143(3)), CPC intimations (143(1)), demand notices, and penalty orders. We prepare detailed replies and represent clients before Assessing Officers.
Should I choose the New or Old Tax Regime?
The optimal choice depends on your income level and available deductions. We do a personalized analysis for every client — comparing both regimes based on your specific situation before recommending the better option.
GST
How long does GST registration take?
GST registration is typically granted within 3–7 working days after submission of complete documents. An ARN is generated immediately upon submission, which can be used for business purposes in the interim.
Is GST registration mandatory for my business?
GST registration is mandatory if your aggregate turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh (goods) or ₹20 lakh (services) in a financial year, or if you are involved in inter-state supply, e-commerce, or as a notified person.
Project Finance
What is CMA data and why do banks require it?
Credit Monitoring Arrangement (CMA) data is a structured financial report banks use to assess a business's creditworthiness for loans. It includes past 3 years financials and 5-year projections. We prepare comprehensive CMA data that meets banker requirements.
What business structure is best for a new venture?
The optimal structure depends on your business goals, number of partners, desired tax treatment, and future plans. A Private Limited Company is ideal for investment-seeking startups; LLPs suit professional service firms; sole proprietorships work for small traders. We advise based on your specific plan.
